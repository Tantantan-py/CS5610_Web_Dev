Game Rules

  1. Objective: Reach the finish line before your opponents by following the game board's paths.
  2. Setup: Each player chooses a token and places it on the starting square.
  3. Gameplay: Players take turns rolling a die and moving forward the corresponding number of spaces.
  4. Special Spaces: Landing on a special space may result in extra turns, setbacks, or bonus points.
  5. Winning: The first player to reach the finish line exactly wins the game. If a roll exceeds the required number, the player remains in place until a valid roll is made.
